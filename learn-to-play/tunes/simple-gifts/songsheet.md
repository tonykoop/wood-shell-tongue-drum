# Simple Gifts — Songsheet (Wood-Shell Tongue Drum)

**Key:** F major  **Tempo:** ♩= 84  **Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆
**Range:** E4–D5  **Source:** Elder Joseph Brackett, Shaker hymn (1848), PD

## Music
```
X:4 T:Simple Gifts M:4/4 L:1/8 Q:1/4=84 K:F
f4 f2 g2 | a4 f4 | a4 b2 c'2 | d'8 |
d'2 c'2 a2 c'2 | d'4 f4 | c'4 a2 f2 | g8 |
(Bb = b in K:F)
```

## Notes
- F major shares all notes with D natural minor — perfectly suited to this drum.
- The warm, woody tone of the instrument suits the simple, humble character of this Shaker hymn.
- The tied final note (f8-) should bloom for 4 full beats.
