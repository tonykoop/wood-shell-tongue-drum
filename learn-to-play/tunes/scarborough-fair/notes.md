# Notes: Scarborough Fair (Adaptation)

**Provenance:** Traditional English ballad, PD. This is an adaptation for D Aeolian —
the traditional melody is D dorian (with B natural, not Bb). The Bb substitution creates
a subtly different modal character while keeping the melody recognizable.

**Range check:** C4–D5. Well within the Standard model's range.

**Practice tips:**
1. The 3/4 waltz pulse should breathe — never rush the spaces between notes.
2. The pickup A (bar 1) enters gently — don't overpower the opening.
3. The Bb substitution (for dorian B natural) is heard most clearly in bars 2 and 10.
