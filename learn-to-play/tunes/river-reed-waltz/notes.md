# Notes: River Reed Waltz

**Composition:** Original waltz for the wood-shell tongue drum, MIT licensed — Heifer Zephyr.
Designed for A3 ding (Standard 16" model). The 3/4 meter suits the gentle rocking of the
wooden vessel's natural sustain.

**Range check:** A3–D5. Full range of the Standard model.

**Practice tips:**
1. The waltz should sway — count 1-2-3 with a slightly emphasized 1 (downbeat).
2. Each phrase ends with a long dotted half note — use this as your breathing point.
3. The transition from phrase 3 (high) to phrase 4 (descending) is the emotional center —
   don't rush the G6 in bar 12; let the audience settle before the descent.
