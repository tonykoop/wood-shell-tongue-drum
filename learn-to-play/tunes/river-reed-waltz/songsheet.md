## Character
A gentle waltz in three: rise, peak, resolve. The opening A3 ding provides the bass anchor;
the melody ascends to D5 at the emotional peak, then spirals back to D below.
The 3/4 waltz suits the wood drum's natural lilt and sustain.

## Notes
- Pickup: two-eighth-note A3 (ding) before bar 1 — a gentle greeting.
- The dotted half notes (D6, G6, F6) = 3 beats; let them ring through the full waltz count.
- The "peak" phrase (bars 9–12): A→C→D→C→A→G — the highest point of the piece.
