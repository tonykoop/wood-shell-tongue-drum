# Forest Walk — Songsheet

**Key:** D natural minor  **Tempo:** ♩= 76  **Meter:** 4/4  **Difficulty:** ★★☆
**Range:** A3–E5  **License:** MIT (original — Heifer Zephyr)

## Music
```
X:2 T:Forest Walk M:4/4 L:1/4 Q:1/4=76 K:Dmin
A, | A, C D E | F2 E D | C D E F | D4 |
E F G A | G2 F E | D E F G | A4 |
A c d e | d2 c A | G F E D | A,4 |
A, | A, D F A | c2 A F | D E F D | A,4 |
```

## Character
Four phrases that gradually ascend and return. The opening A3 ding pickup sets a grounded
pace; each phrase rises a step higher before returning to earth. The final phrase is
a D minor arpeggio (A D F A) that resolves back to the ding.

## Notes
- The pickup A3 (A,) is the ding — start with a single confident ding stroke.
- The gradual ascent (phrases 1→3) builds energy; phrase 4 is the resolution.
- At ♩= 76, this should feel like a moderate walking pace — unhurried but forward.
