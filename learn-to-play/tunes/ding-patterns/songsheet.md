# Ding Drone Patterns — Songsheet

**Tempo:** Free  **Meter:** Free  **Difficulty:** ★☆☆

## Three meditative patterns
1. Ding → 5th (A→E): strike ding, wait, play E. Repeat 8×.
2. Ding → scale walk: ding + ascending D minor scale up and down.
3. Call and response: ding "asks," single high note "answers."

**Duration:** 5–10 minutes (as long as feels right)

These patterns are not tunes — they are listening exercises.
The goal is to hear the wood vessel resonate, not to play a melody.
