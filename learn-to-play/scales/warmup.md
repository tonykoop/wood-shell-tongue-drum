# Wood-Shell Tongue Drum Warm-Up

## Ding exploration (beginner)
Strike the ding (center A3 or D ding) and listen to its full decay before moving on.
Then play each tongue field gently in sequence, ascending from lowest to highest.

```
X:1
T:Scale Walk (D natural minor from A ding)
M:4/4
L:1/4
Q:1/4=66
K:Dmin
A, B, C D | E F G A | A G F E | D C B, A, |
```

## Ding-field interval pairs
```
X:2
T:Ding Plus Field
M:4/4
L:1/2
Q:1/4=60
K:Dmin
A, A | A, C | A, D | A, E | A, F | A, G | A, A |
```

**Tip:** The wood shell resonates in sympathy with the ding. After each pair,
pause and listen for the sympathetic resonance fading — that's your cue to proceed.
